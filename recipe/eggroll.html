Eggroll

Description

This dish is filled with enough crispy rolls that will melt your taste buds. These egg rolls are filled to the brim with meat and vegetables, and almost entirely identical from what you would get at a restaurant. It's thick crispy fried bread surrounding its delicious insides are fried in hot oil and served warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 teaspoon ground giner, or more to taste
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 2 ounces shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 quart peanut oil for frying
  • 8(7 inch square) egg roll wrappers
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds (optional)
