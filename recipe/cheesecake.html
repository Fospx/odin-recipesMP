Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a dessert that is known for its rich and creamy texture. It is usually made with cookies or graham crackers and filling of cream cheese. Cheesecakes can be chilled or baked, in which results in a smooth melting flavor in your mouth. Many toppings can be used such as fruit and chocolate, or an extra layer of whipped cream. Whether it is enjoyed plain or with crazy toppings, cheesecake is a desert that engulfs the taste buds with enjoyable flavors.
